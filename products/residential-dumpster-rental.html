Residential dumpster rentals typically range from $299-$599 depending on size, location, and rental period. Our 10-yard dumpsters start around $299, while 20-yard dumpsters average $399-$499. All prices include delivery, pickup, disposal fees, and a generous weight allowance. Call 888-478-DUMP for an exact quote for your area.

For most whole-home cleanouts, we recommend a 20-yard dumpster. It holds about 8-10 pickup truck loads of material. For smaller projects like a single room or garage cleanout, a 10-yard dumpster is usually sufficient. Our experts can help you choose the right size - just call us and describe your project.

Yes! Most residential customers place dumpsters in their driveway. We'll provide boards to protect your driveway surface. If you prefer street placement, you may need a permit from your local municipality - we can help guide you through that process.
